Paola Di Maio, PhD

Systems Engineering · Knowledge Representation · Consciousness Studies · Citizen Science

I work at the intersection of systems thinking, contemplative practice, and human-AI co-evolution, treating technological development as both rigorous research and a practice in awareness.

My approach integrates three decades of systems engineering with Buddhist contemplative traditions, particularly Dzogchen, exploring how consciousness and technology inform each other. Based in Taiwan's countryside, I balance intensive research with embodied practice.

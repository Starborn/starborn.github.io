About

I work at the intersection of systems thinking, contemplative practice, and human-AI co-evolution, treating technological development as both rigorous research and a practice in awareness.

My approach integrates systems engineering with diverse disciplines, exploring how intelligence, consciousness and technology inform each other. I balance intensive research with embodied practice.
